What I Do

AI-Enhanced Quality

I build and integrate AI-powered tools to revolutionize quality engineering. This includes creating custom AI agents and leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) for intelligent test data generation, automated code analysis, and predictive quality insights.

My expertise extends to developing and deploying these solutions on specialized infrastructure like MCP (Multi-Agent Communication Platform) servers, enabling scalable and robust multi-agent systems that drive efficiency and uncover complex issues traditional testing might miss.

Technical Leadership

I build and scale high-performing engineering teams that deliver quality at speed. My approach combines technical mentorship with strategic guidance, helping teams adopt best practices in automation, DevOps, TestOps, and continuous delivery.

I implement DORA metrics and custom dashboards that enable data-driven decision making, creating visibility and accountability across engineering organizations. My data obsession ensures quality is measurable, actionable, and continuously improving across the development lifecycle.

Test Automation

I design and implement robust automation frameworks using modern tools like Playwright, Cypress, Detox, k6 and custom solutions for E2E, API, Mobile, and Performance testing. My implementations achieve 90%+ test coverage, reliable CI/CD pipelines, and efficient test environments.

I've developed specialized security testing tools including custom SAST and FOSS scanners that integrate seamlessly into development workflows and leverage AI for intelligent test prioritization and predictive quality analytics.
